Summary

Mid-career air quality engineer with 5 years of experience addressing real-world environmental issues through emissions quantification, analysis, and communication. Combines strong background in chemical engineering principles, data science, and practical experience to quantify emissions from transportation, power generation, and chemical manufacturing activities. Proficient in using Python to research and advise implementation of policy goals for governmental agencies. Motivated to improve the air quality and public health of at-risk communities through sound science and policy.
